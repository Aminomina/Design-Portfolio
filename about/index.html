• About Me

    My name is Anna Lugthart, I’m a Mechanical Engineer, and I’m passionate about design! I'm endlessly fascinated by engineering, art, writing, and honestly most other things I try. Above all, I love learning.

    I had the privilege of studying at Northwestern University, where I learned engineering principles that extended the reach of my creativity. During that time, I also worked at the Ford Prototyping Lab where I gained valuable skills in fabrication.

    Over the years I’ve worked on many design projects and other creative endeavors. Some were for work, some were for school, and some were just for me. Many of those projects can be found right here in this portfolio.

    About this Portfolio

    I coded this portfolio from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. After taking a web development course, I decided to design a portfolio in order to practice my learnings in the context of a useful project.

    I aim to maintain modular, easy-to-read code that can evolve along with my needs for this site. Except where otherwise notated, all code was authored by me.
